What is SummarizeIt?

SummarizeIt is an indexing search platform based on WikipediaAPI-python and Wikipedia-Search-Tool modules and APIs. Having its web version based on a python Backend with Flask and a Frontend based on JavaScript and Bootstrap-CSS. Through the SummarizeIt platform it is possible to search for any article that is available within the Wikipedia database and receive, through this, a summary/synthesis of it.

To know more deeply about the project, go click on the "Project" link on the top navigation bar.
